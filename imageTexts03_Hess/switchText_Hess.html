Looking out, wishing to be seen

Calling out, wishing to be heard

She is praise and fear and unknowns

That a soft spotlight illuminates.

She finds her breath

In something taking careful shape;

Her portrait in soul,

And she can't look away.

Sight stained with beauty, never erased,

Forged by her hand from the blood in her veins.

Insides out for all to see, she's emptied out but now at least

She can finally hear it singing.
